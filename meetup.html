Top 5 ways to Deal with Difficult People

Date 7th Mar 2020
Time 10:00 am - 12:00 pm
Venue Dialogues Cafe
J P Nagar, Bengaluru
Fee INR 500/-

It can be frustrating to deal with an authoritative boss at work who simply wont get off your back. Or, it could be a personal relationship in which you repeatedly find it difficult to get through to someone.

We all face the challenges that come with dealing with difficult people. Some people who might be demanding or come into the relationship with a sense of entitlement. We try out different things to protect ourselves - sometimes lash back, sometimes bottle it all up inside. In spite of that, there is no one way that seems to work perfectly resulting in confusion. What was the best way to deal with this anyway?

Join us for a meetup as we decode the best ways to deal with the difficult relationships in our life and how to come out unhurt through such situations.

Key Takeaways:

  • How to deal with people who are authoritative
  • How to set boundaries with people
  • How to Gain compliance from people in personal and professional relationships
  • How to Communicate effortlessly and increase our chances of influencing
