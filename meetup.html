Design your own Reality

Date 1st Sep 2019
Time 10:00 am - 12:00 pm
Venue Dialogues Cafe
J P Nagar, Bengaluru
Fee INR 200/-

Do things just happen to us? Or are we unconsciously choosing events that we experience in our daily lives?

Join us for a meetup to find out answers to more such questions. We will have a talk followed by a discussion on how our beliefs play out in our daily lives and what can be done to change it if we want to.

Key Takeaways:

  • How does our reality get created?
  • How do highly successful people represent the world to themselves?
  • How to gain control over what happens with us?
  • Our Inner Representation of the Outer world
  • Our Unconscious Programming
