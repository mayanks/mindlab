Building High Self-esteem & Confidence

Date 9th May 2020, Saturday
Time 11:00 am - 12:00 pm
Location Online

How we think and feel about ourselves shows up in our communication and our behavior with others. It is a powerful communicator that mostly operates at an underlying level and dictates how people perceive us. Sometimes while dealing with difficult people we could come to believe that their behavior is about us. However, is it always so?

Join us for a webinar on Saturday where we will discuss what impacts our self-esteem and how we can build a strong, unshakable image of ourselves.

Key Takeaways:
  • What is Self-esteem?
  • Top Indicators of High Self-esteem
  • How to build a strong Self-image
  • Key to generating confidence in difficult situations
